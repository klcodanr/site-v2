I was watching the dark clouds and drinking my half-full cup of coffee when I stumbled across a reddit thread complaining about React. Naturally, feeling combative, I replied that:

> Most frameworks are bad.

Quite reasonably, a few people replied that I wasn't considering Unfortunately, I'd stumbled on the same pitfall that that drives one of the hardest challenges in being a parent: using the right level of specificity.

 Don't hit your sister? Don't hit people. Don't hit anything. Yes you can hit your drum, etc.   
  
  
 Similarly, React is bad. All frameworks are bad. All software is bad. Well except the software you need.
