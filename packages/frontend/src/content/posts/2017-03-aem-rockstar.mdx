I’m thrilled to announce that I will be presenting at Adobe Summit during the [AEM Rockstars session](https://adobesummit.lanyonevents.com/2017/connect/sessionDetail.ww?SESSION%5FID=3985). This session will feature 5 AEM Rockstars presenting the top-voted tips, tricks and strategies from this year.

![](/images/posts/2017-03-13-aem-rockstar/aem_rockstar-600x384.png)

I will be presenting on using Sling Models to integrate Adobe Experience Manager and the Adobe Marketing Cloud. This talk will include the presentation of an open source library for teams to easily expose their data in AEM to the Adobe Marketing Cloud.

If you are coming to [Adobe Summit 2017](https://summit.adobe.com/na/), join us at the [AEM Rockstars](https://adobesummit.lanyonevents.com/2017/connect/sessionDetail.ww?SESSION%5FID=3985)!
