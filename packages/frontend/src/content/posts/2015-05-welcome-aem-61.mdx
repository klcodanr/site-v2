### What's in AEM 6.1?

 The AEM 6.1 release includes, among many new features and enhancements, deeper integration into the Adobe Marketing Cloud.

[ ![Adobe Marketing Cloud](/images/posts/2015-05-28-welcome-aem-61/adobe-marketing-cloud.jpg) ](http://www.adobe.com/marketing-cloud/online-marketing-solutions "Adobe Marketing Cloud Solutions") 

 This latest release also enhances and demarcates the individual components of AEM and adds some whole new functionalities.

[ ![AEM Components](/images/posts/2015-05-28-welcome-aem-61/aem-components.jpg) ](http://www.adobe.com/marketing-cloud/enterprise-content-management/digital-experience-capabilities "AEM 6.1 Capabilities")
